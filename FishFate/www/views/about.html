How do the fish make a random number?

  1. Fish Setup
  2. Fish Tracking


    Fish movements are tracked by their initial position on an x/y plane, where their next position they move to will generate a binary number as shown on the the picture above.
  3. Generating Randomness

    We read in a certain number of bits from the bitstream and then calculate the distribution of the 1's and 0's.

    From that we calculate the Shannon Entropy which tells the amount of entropy in the bits. Entropy is then calculated until there are 256 bits of entropy to be inputted into a SHA1 Hash Function.

    The hash function then outputs a 160 bit Hex number and finally this Hex number is converted to a Binary number to send to the server.
